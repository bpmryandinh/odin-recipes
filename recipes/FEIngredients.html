Ingredients


Fried Egg Recipe
  • Egg: I tested this recipe using large chicken eggs. Of course, you can use medium, small, or jumbo-sized, but just be aware that you may need to adjust cooking times accordingly.
  • Butter: I like to cook my eggs in a little unsalted butter. It browns as the eggs cook, imparting this gorgeous slightly nutty flavor to the edges; however, you can also use a neutral oil, if you prefer.
  • Salt: I use kosher salt to season my eggs, but you can also use table salt or fine sea salt if that’s what you have on hand.
  • Ground Pepper: I love freshly ground pepper on an egg. Feel free to leave it out if you prefer, and if it’s not freshly ground, I’d recommend skipping altogether.
